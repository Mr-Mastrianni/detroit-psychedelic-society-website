Detroit Psychedelic Society

Brand Identity Guidelines

A comprehensive guide to our visual identity, voice, and mission as we foster consciousness exploration, community connection, and educational enlightenment through the lens of Detroit's unique cultural renaissance.

Brand Overview

Since being established in 2014, The Detroit Psychedelic Society has been known to push the envelope in moving forward all positive energy as it relates to psychedelics. Our identity merges the industrial heritage of Detroit with the organic, flowing nature of psychedelic exploration.

This brand identity document serves as a guide for all communications, ensuring a consistent representation of our values, mission, and aesthetic across all touchpoints. We blend sacred geometry with Detroit's urban landscape to create a unique visual language that honors both our roots and our vision.

Our Mission

"To foster community healing, personal growth, and educational enlightenment through the responsible exploration of psychedelic experiences, while honoring Detroit's unique cultural renaissance."

Typography Guidelines

Maintain clear hierarchy with consistent font sizing. Use Montserrat for headings, titles, and important callouts. Poppins works for body text, descriptions, and interface elements. This combination creates a balance between the bold, visionary aspects of our mission and the accessible, educational focus of our work.

Core Values

Educational Excellence

Information and education always come first. We commit to providing accurate, evidence-based knowledge that empowers individuals to make informed decisions about psychedelic exploration.

Community Connection

We foster meaningful relationships and collective growth through shared experiences, collaborative learning, and mutual support, reflecting Detroit's strong community traditions.

Responsible Exploration

Safety, consent, and ethical consideration guide all our practices. We emphasize harm reduction, respect for sacred traditions, and thoughtful integration of experiences.

Inclusive Accessibility

We welcome diverse backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences, creating spaces where everyone feels valued regardless of race, gender, age, or prior experience with psychedelics.

Cultural Integration

We honor Detroit's rich history and present renaissance, integrating local arts, music, and cultural expressions into our approach to psychedelic exploration and community building.

Scientific Grounding

We embrace evidence-based approaches and contribute to the growing body of research on psychedelics, balancing ancestral wisdom with contemporary science.

Target Audience

The Detroit Psychedelic Society serves a diverse community united by curiosity, openness to growth, and connection to Detroit's unique cultural landscape.

Conscious Explorers

Individuals seeking personal growth, healing, and expanded consciousness through intentional psychedelic experiences. They value education, preparation, and integration practices.

Community Builders

People who see the potential for psychedelic experiences to strengthen community bonds and foster collective healing, particularly in the context of Detroit's revitalization.

Knowledge Seekers

Researchers, students, healthcare professionals, and the intellectually curious who want evidence-based information about psychedelics, regardless of their interest in personal use.

Detroit Cultural Catalysts

Artists, musicians, creators, and visionaries who see the connection between psychedelic exploration and Detroit's artistic renaissance, seeking to integrate these experiences into their creative practice.

Healing-Focused Individuals

People exploring alternative approaches to mental health, trauma recovery, addiction, and personal transformation, often after conventional approaches have proven insufficient.

Brand Voice & Messaging

Grounded yet Visionary

We balance scientific approach with spiritual exploration, maintaining credibility while embracing the transcendent aspects of psychedelic experiences.

"Our workshop combines evidence-based protocols with space for personal revelation, honoring both the science and the mystery of psilocybin."

Inclusive & Welcoming

Our language invites participation from all backgrounds and experience levels, avoiding jargon while still honoring the depth of psychedelic traditions.

"Whether this is your first exploration or you're continuing a lifelong journey, you'll find community and wisdom in our integration circle."

Safety-First Messaging

We emphasize responsible exploration, preparation, and integration in all communications, never glamorizing use without context.

"Preparation is essential for meaningful experiences. Our harm reduction workshop provides the foundation needed for safe exploration."

Community-Centered

Our voice highlights collective experience and Detroit's unique psychedelic culture, emphasizing connection over individualism.

"Detroit's renaissance mirrors our collective inner journeys—rebuilding, reconnecting, and reimagining what's possible when we come together."

Communication Principles

  • Always provide context for psychedelic discussions
  • Balance accessibility with depth of information
  • Honor indigenous and traditional knowledge while avoiding appropriation
  • Acknowledge legal realities while advocating for evidence-based policy
  • Use inclusive language that welcomes diverse perspectives
  • Connect Detroit's cultural identity to broader psychedelic movements

Distinctive Detroit Elements

Urban Renewal Metaphors

We draw parallels between Detroit's rebirth and personal transformation, using the city's resilience as inspiration for inner growth and healing.

Industrial-Organic Fusion

Our visual language merges Detroit's industrial heritage with organic, flowing psychedelic motifs—steel and concrete intertwined with mycelia and vines.

Detroit Color Influences

Our palette incorporates both urban landscapes (deep blues of night skylines) and Michigan's natural environments (greens of forests, blues of Great Lakes).

Community Spotlights

We showcase Detroit's diverse psychedelic pioneers and community leaders, centering local voices and experiences in our educational materials.

Michigan-Specific Context

Our communications acknowledge and educate about Michigan's psychedelic legal landscape, including decriminalization efforts in Detroit, Ann Arbor, Hazel Park, Ferndale, and Ypsilanti.
